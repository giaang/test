How to bake bread in 50 minutes

You need just 50 minutes to bake a delicious soda bread! No yeast, no need to wait!

What you need

  • 1 kg flour
  • 700 ml buttermilk
  • 2 teaspoons soda
  • salt

How to proceed

  1. Sieve flour and soda.
  2. Add buttermilk and mix to make a soft dough.
  3. Cut a cross over the top.
  4. Preheat the oven at 200°C.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes.
